Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted with Passion

The Chennai Chappals was born from a simple yet profound idea to craft footwear that pays homage to the rich cultural heritage of Tiruvannamalai while meeting the demands of modern urban life. What started as a humble endeavor has evolved into a brand that is now synonymous with quality, style, and tradition. Our journey has been one of passion, unwavering dedication, and a steadfast commitment to excellence.


At The Chennai Chappals, we celebrate a timeless tradition of craftsmanship and quality. Based in the heart of Chennai, we are dedicated to creating exquisite chappals that blend classic design with modern comfort. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the rich heritage of traditional Indian footwear into the contemporary world, maintaining the elegance and durability that our ancestors cherished

Our Mission

Crafting Footwear that Speaks to the Soul

We are dedicated to delivering handcrafted footwear that combines the best of tradition and innovation. Our mission is to create products that resonate with our customers, offering comfort, style, and a connection to the cultural roots of Chennai.
